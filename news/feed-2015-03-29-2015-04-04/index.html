OpenFastStruct

On 2015-04-02

I have built a new Ruby gem after YARR.

Introducing OpenFastStruct. OpenFastStruct is a data structure, similar to an OpenStruct, that allows the definition of arbitrary attributes with their accompanying values. It benchmarks ~3x slower than a Hash, but it’s ~4x faster than OpenStruct.
